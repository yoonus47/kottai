    About Us

    The Kottai family is a traditional Malabar Muslim family primarily situated in the Edathiruthy Panchayath, Thrissur District of Kerala. For generations, the majority of family members have been engaged in agriculture within the local community. This website aims to function as an information hub for the family's traditions and values, while also serving as a knowledge repository for family members to connect and get to know one another.
